Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-27

Velox (“we”, “us”, “our”) lets you upscale short videos using cloud processing. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account data (email) if you sign in via Firebase Authentication.
  • Purchase data from RevenueCat to verify subscriptions (we don’t receive your full card details).
  • Diagnostics such as crash and error logs (if enabled) to improve stability.
  • Job metadata for uploaded videos (e.g., filename, size, processing status) to deliver results and show history.

How we use information

  • Provide and operate the app (authenticate, process jobs, save results).
  • Validate purchases and restore access across devices.
  • Maintain and improve performance, reliability, and support.

Sharing

We use service providers acting on our behalf: Google Firebase (auth, database, storage), RevenueCat (in-app purchases), and our cloud processing provider. Their use of data is governed by their respective policies and our instructions.

Retention

We retain account, purchase, and job data as needed to provide the service and meet legal requirements. You may request deletion as described below.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. If you are in a region with specific privacy rights, you may exercise them by contacting us.
