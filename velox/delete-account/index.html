Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: 2025-10-04

If you would like to delete your Velox account and associated data, follow the instructions below. You can also request deletion if you have already uninstalled the app.

How to request deletion

  • Email us at support@opallabsdev.com with the subject “Velox account deletion”.
  • Send the request from the email address linked to your account, and (if available) include your Firebase UID or in-app user ID.

We’ll confirm receipt and notify you when the deletion is complete.

What we delete

  • Account identifiers (e.g., Firebase UID, profile document)
  • Job data in Firestore (e.g., video job records, statuses, timestamps)
  • Uploaded videos and processed results stored in Firebase Storage

What may be retained (limited & time-bound)

We may retain minimal operational logs and payment records as required for security, prevention of abuse, accounting, or legal obligations, for up to 90 days after completion of your deletion request.

Timeline

We aim to complete deletion within 7 days (and no later than 30 days). You’ll receive a confirmation email when finished.

Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel a subscription. To cancel, open the Google Play Store app: Profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions, select Velox, and tap Cancel. Your access will continue until the current billing period ends.

Self-service deletions (optional)

If available in your version of the app, you can manually delete individual video jobs or results in the app. For full account deletion, please also email us as described above.
